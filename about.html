About Us

Our Story

ENZA PETROLEUM is an African-owned fuel supply company committed to powering South Africa's future. We take pride in being a compliant and trusted partner for businesses across the nation.

Founded with a vision to provide reliable and efficient fuel solutions, we have grown to become a leading supplier of quality petroleum products to various industries.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and environmental responsibility sets us apart in the industry. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients by delivering exceptional service and innovative solutions.
